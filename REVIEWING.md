# Open Review Guide

## Purpose

This project is open for critical review. Agreement with the paper's
interpretation is not a condition of participation. The most useful responses
identify what was checked, where it appears, and why the observation matters.

The current reader-facing manuscript is version 1.1.0, archived as a Zenodo
working paper under DOI
[10.5281/zenodo.21459465](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21459465). Its analysis
code, data, and source files are preserved in the corresponding
[reproducibility package](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21450852).

The paper presents the patterns and leaves their larger significance for the
reader to consider. Review is therefore welcome from readers who approach the
material mathematically, textually, historically, theologically, or from a
combination of those perspectives.

## Useful Kinds of Review

- **Arithmetic and reproducibility:** Recalculate a value, rerun a script, or
  inspect whether a reported count follows from the declared rules.
- **Hebrew text and sources:** Check spelling, word boundaries, vocalization
  policy, source witnesses, transliteration, or the status of a proposed name,
  title, or phrase.
- **Method and controls:** Examine corpus boundaries, comparison sets,
  multiple-testing concerns, sensitivity analyses, or possible selection
  effects.
- **Interpretation:** Offer a theological, historical, or literary reading,
  including reasonable alternatives to the paper's discussion.
- **Clarity:** Identify a passage whose structure, terminology, or distinction
  between calculation and interpretation could be made easier to follow.

## Where to Respond

- Use the repository's
  [open-review discussion](https://github.com/cammackj/numerical-architecture-divine-names/discussions/1)
  for broad responses, questions, interpretations, and possible extensions.
- Open an [issue](https://github.com/cammackj/numerical-architecture-divine-names/issues/new/choose)
  for a specific arithmetic, transcription, citation, source, or reproducibility
  problem that can be investigated and resolved.

When possible, include the manuscript page or section, the exact text or value
being discussed, the steps used to check it, and a source or reproducible
calculation. It also helps to identify the response as one of four things:
`correction`, `ambiguity`, `alternative interpretation`, or `possible extension`.

## Version Integrity

Published PDFs and numbered releases will not be silently replaced. Confirmed
corrections and accepted additions will be documented in `CHANGELOG.md` and,
when appropriate, incorporated into a new numbered version under the rules in
[`docs/UPDATE_POLICY.md`](docs/UPDATE_POLICY.md). A suggestion can be recorded
and discussed without being added to the manuscript.

Reviewers may cite either the version DOI above for the exact manuscript or the
working-paper concept DOI
[10.5281/zenodo.21459464](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21459464) for the
continuing publication.
