# Ehyeh Outer-Pair 42 Formal Review

## Decision

Retain the observation in manuscript version 1.2.0, with explicit
limits. The arithmetic, textual boundary, and traditional reference are all
valid. The manuscript must describe 42 as the subtotal contributed by the two
repeated outer occurrences of Ehyeh, not as the value of the complete
declaration and not as an identification with the separate forty-two-letter
Name tradition.

Earlier numbered releases were not modified during this review.

## Source Boundary

Exodus 3:14 contains the exact three-word declaration:

`Ehyeh Asher Ehyeh / אהיה אשר אהיה`

The first and third words are therefore a textually defined repeated frame.
Adding them is not an arbitrary choice of two unrelated words, although the
numerical significance of their subtotal was noticed after inspection.

Primary source:

- https://www.sefaria.org/Exodus.3.14?lang=bi

## Arithmetic

Under standard gematria:

- `Ehyeh / אהיה = 1 + 5 + 10 + 5 = 21`;
- the repeated outer pair contributes `21 + 21 = 42`;
- `Asher / אשר = 1 + 300 + 200 = 501`;
- the complete declaration is `21 + 501 + 21 = 543 = 393_12`.

Thus 42 is an exact compositional subtotal. It is not the complete phrase
value, which remains 543.

## Traditional Reference

Kiddushin 71a:13 refers to a forty-two-letter Name. That object is identified
by letter count and guarded transmission, not by the standard gematria of the
English label or by the subtotal in Exodus 3:14.

Primary source:

- https://www.sefaria.org/Kiddushin.71a.13?lang=bi

The defensible statement is therefore a resonance between three distinct
contacts with 42:

1. Eloah has the exact standard-gematria value 42.
2. The repeated Ehyeh frame contributes the exact subtotal 42.
3. A separate rabbinic tradition refers to a Name of forty-two letters.

The paper does not claim that these are the same object or that one derives
from another.

## Control Boundary

The synthetic Asher test explains the arithmetic mechanism as `2x + 501`.
Eighteen matched surface forms across ten consonant families reproduce the
complete value `393_12`, all through outer expressions with value 21. At the
distinct-value level, 53 of 786 tested outer values produce some base-12
palindrome.

This prevents a claim that the arithmetic completion is unique to Ehyeh. It
does not supply another attested divine self-declaration with the same exact
`X-Asher-X` structure. The complete scriptural expression remains the textual
object of interest.

## Approved Wording Rules

- Say that the two repeated outer occurrences contribute or subtotal 42.
- Keep the complete value `543_10 = 393_12` visible nearby.
- Identify the forty-two-letter Name as a separate traditional object.
- Describe the connection as compositional or interpretive, not as an
  arithmetic transition law or identity.
- Do not assign a prospective probability to the post-inspection subtotal.
